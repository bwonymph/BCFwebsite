The Venue

We're hosting at Chevron Auditorium at the International House in Berkeley.

    Free Street Parking: Free spaces along and adjacent to Piedmont have 2 hour maximums Monday-Saturday 9am-7pm.
    Meters: Bancroft Ave. meters accept major credit cards 9am-6pm Monday-Saturday. Free Sundays and after 6pm.
    Public Lots: Next to the Berkeley Art Museum - Bancroft & Bowditch.
    Campus Lots: During traditional work hours, hourly parking, paid in advance Maxwell Garage (#4), on the corner of Gayley and Stadium Rim Way, a 3 minute walk from I-House or Underhill Lot (#1) at the corner of Channing & College, a 5-10 minute walk from I-House.
    After 5pm Monday-Friday and weekends, hourly public parking is available in all campus parking areas unless otherwise indicated.
    Lots closest to IH are: Boalt Law School Lot ( #2, behind the building opposite I-House) Kroeber Lot (#3, on Bancroft near College Ave. under tennis courts.)
    BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit) The Downtown Berkeley BART station is closest to I-House but be aware the 20- 25 minute walk is on an uphill incline. Taxis are available. Rockridge BART station is convenient by bus transfer to the 51A that runs along College Avenue.
